Raliul Maramureșului

With a history spanning 54 years, the rallies held in Maramureș have had names that have become emblematic for this type of automobile competition: the Chestnut Rally, the Danube Rally, the Romanian Rally, the Siromex Rally, the Maramureș Rally. Since the first rally, held in 1971, Gutin has been an integral part and has become the rally's queen stage. At this year's edition, athletes will once again race on the legendary stage. After the premiere in 2024, this year too, the return from Țara Maramureș will be run at night. The two crossings over Gutin will be separated by a Superspecial stage held in Sighetu Marmației.
